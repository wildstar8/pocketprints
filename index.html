About Me

Hi, I'm Saanvi!

I'm an 11-year-old maker and the founder of Pocket Prints!

My 3D printing journey started when I was 8 years old and got a 3D printer as a birthday gift. From the moment I saw a real object appear layer by layer out of nothing, I was hooked. It felt like magic — and I've been creating ever since.

I started Pocket Prints because I wanted to make fun, pocket-sized toys and trinkets that kids would actually love carrying around. Every clicker, fidget toy, and bookmark I sell is printed by me, right at home, using professionally designed files I'm licensed to print and sell.

When I'm not printing, you can find me reading, playing the piano, playing sports, or doing science experiments with my fish cheering me on from his tank.

🐟 Fish owner 🔬 Science lover 🎹 Piano player ⚽ Sports fan 📚 Bookworm
